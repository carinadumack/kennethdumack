Protists shape the wastewater microbiome. I investigate biotic interactions among the vast microbial diversity in wastewater. For this, I use -omics techniques, bioinformatics, and also microscopy-based approaches.

Selected research

Parasite removal

An important task of wastewater treatment is the removal of parasites to suppress distribution of potentially infectious organisms into the environment. We investigate whether, when, and how these parasites are removed.
