Protistan diversity is yet largely unexplored. With novel technology, we are finally able to rapidly investigate the diversity and evolution of protists. My research focuses on the exploration of the Rhizaria, a poorly-known taxon as diverse as, for instance, the plants. Many Rhizaria feed on other eukaryotes or evolved astonishing parasite-like adaptions to their prey.
